COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about joining the league.

When are practices held?

Typically Tuesday and Thursday evenings from 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM at our main arena.

Can I watch a practice?

Yes! We have an observation deck for prospective skaters and fans. Please check in at the front desk upon arrival.

What equipment do I need to start?

You'll need quad skates, a helmet, knee pads, elbow pads, wrist guards, and a mouthguard. We offer rental gear for your first three sessions!
