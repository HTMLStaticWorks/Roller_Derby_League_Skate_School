THE ANATOMY OF A BOUT

Understanding the game is the first step to mastering it.

01

THE JAMMER

The star on the helmet. Jammers are the point-scorers, sprinting through the pack and gaining points for every opposing blocker they pass. Speed, agility, and endurance are their primary weapons.

02

THE BLOCKERS

The wall of defense. Four blockers from each team work together to stop the opposing jammer while simultaneously helping their own jammer through. It's a high-speed game of physical chess.

03

THE PIVOT

The strategist. Recognizable by the stripe on their helmet, the pivot is a blocker who can take over the jammer role if needed. They lead the pack and coordinate the defensive formations on the fly.
