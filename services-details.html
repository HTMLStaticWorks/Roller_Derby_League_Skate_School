FRESH MEAT BASICS

The foundation of every great derby athlete.

PROGRAM OVERVIEW

Our Fresh Meat program is designed for individuals who have never stepped on a pair of quad skates before. Over 12 weeks, we transform you into a confident skater ready for contact.

You'll learn the essential 27 minimum skills required by the WFTDA, including cross-overs, T-stops, transitions, and falling safely without injuring yourself or others.

WHAT YOU'LL LEARN

A breakdown of our 12-week intensive curriculum.

Weeks 1-4

  • Balance & Form
  • Proper Falling
  • T-Stops & Plough Stops

Weeks 5-8

  • Cross-overs
  • Transitions
  • Backward Skating

Weeks 9-12

  • Intro to Hitting
  • Track Awareness
  • Mock Scrimmages

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Do I need my own skates?

We provide rental gear for the first 4 weeks, but we recommend purchasing your own quad skates for the best experience.

Is it dangerous?

Roller derby is a full-contact sport, but we prioritize safety. You'll learn to fall safely before you learn to hit.

What is the age limit?

Our Fresh Meat program is for 18+. We have a separate Youth Academy for younger skaters.
